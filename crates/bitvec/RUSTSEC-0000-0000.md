# bitvec is unmaintained

At time of reporting, the most recent version of the `bitvec` package (`1.0.1`) was published
to crates.io on 2022-07-10, more than three years in the past.

The last `main` branch commit visible in GitHub was on 2023-04-12 and there is no meaningful
activity by maintainers visible in the [issue tracker](https://github.com/ferrilab/bitvec/issues).
There are also unconfirmed [reports of possible unsoundness](https://github.com/ferrilab/bitvec/issues/283).

## Known alternatives

Depending on the required functionality, the `bit-vec` package may be a suitable replacement,
though it offers a significantly smaller feature set.
